Where's the delta?▋

Task — summarize a deal memo for an MD's Monday update.
Lazy 
summarize this memo
→ output
The memo covers a potential transaction and outlines several considerations, risks, and next steps for the team to review.
Engineered 
You're a senior analyst prepping an MD for Monday.
Summarize the memo as 3 bullets — what changed,
why it matters, the risk — plus one next step.
Under 80 words. Plain English, no jargon.
→ output
• Changed: target raised the ask to 9.5× EBITDA. • Matters: drops projected IRR below our 18% floor. • Risk: a rival bidder is circling — window ~2 weeks. Next: re-run the model at 8.5×, flag the deal team today.
role · structure · format · a hard limit

Lever 02 / 04

Show, don't tell.

One example of "good" beats three paragraphs of instructions. (Few-shot, in plain terms.)

The real unlock

Manage what it knows.

  • Feed it the right snippet — not the whole drive.
  • The wrong context beats no context. Curate ruthlessly.
  • Know when to start fresh. The chat isn't sacred.
